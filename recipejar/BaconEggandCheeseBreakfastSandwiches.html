Bacon Egg and Cheese Breakfast Sandwiches

Notes:

Inspired by McDonald's. Their food sucks, but their imagination is hard to top.

You will need:

  • Bacon at least 2 slices per person
  • Eggs at least 1 per person.
  • American Cheese
  • Maple syrup

Procedure:

Make pancakes, consider including the syrup in the batter. Fry up the bacon and eggs(as desired, but over hard ones won't be as messy).
Layer:

  1. Pancake

  2. Syrup

  3. Fried Egg

  4. 2 strips of Bacon

  5. Slice of American Cheese

  6. more Syrup

  7. Pancake



Serve warm.
