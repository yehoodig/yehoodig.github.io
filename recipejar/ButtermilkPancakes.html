Buttermilk Pancakes

Notes:

Started with the Joy of Cooking recipe, and modified it. Usually enough for 3-4 people.
You need 1/4 tsp Baking soda, for every half cup buttermilk.
Yogurt can be substituted for the buttermilk 1:1, but the batter will be very thick. You
could dilute it with milk, but if you do that, you might want to add 1/4 tsp baking soda to compensate for the extra volume.

You will need:

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/3 Cup sugar(optional, with enough, you don't even need syrup)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Cups buttermilk
  • 3-4 Tbsps melted butter

Procedure:

Mix dry and wet(including melted butter) ingredients seperately. Then mix together, but not too much, a few lumps are okay.


Cook in a preheated pan or griddle on medium low. No grease necessary if the melted butter is used.
