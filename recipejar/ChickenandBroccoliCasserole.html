Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

Notes:

From betterrecipes.com.

You will need:

  • 2 pkgs frozen cut broccoli
  • 4 Chicken Breasts, cooked and cooled
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 can condensed cream of celery soup
  • 1/2 Cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 Cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp worchestershire sauce (to taste)
  • 1/2 Cup heavy whipping cream, whipped
  • Cooked rice

Procedure:

Cook Broccoli. Place in greased 13x9. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Remove chicken from skin and bone, cut into pieces. Add to baking dish. Heat together soup, milk and nutmeg. Add mayo, Worchestershire sauce and cream. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. Bake for 20 min at 4°F00 degrees. Serve over rice.
