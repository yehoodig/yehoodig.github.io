Ecuadorian Salad

Notes:

You will need:

  • Red Onions
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemons/lemon juice

Procedure:

Slice thinly, toss together, drown with lemon juice, let set for about 20 min, and enjoy.
