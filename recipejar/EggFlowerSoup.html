Egg Flower Soup

Notes:

I may have gotten this from the Joy of Cooking,
but I don't remember.

You will need:

  • 1 egg
  • 2 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 Tbsp Corn Starch

Procedure:

Mix cornstarch into cold water, or broth. Heat to boiling and then allow to cool.


Heat the broth in a medium sauce pan until almost,
but not quite boiling. Beat the egg until frothy.
Then, slowly stir the broth and very slowly pour the
egg in. The egg should cook and "flower".
