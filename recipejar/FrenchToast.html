French Toast

Notes:

Just got lucky.

You will need:

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2-1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1-2 Tbsps Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/3 Cup Milk
  • 3-4 Tbsps Melted Butter

Procedure:

Whip it all together, dunk bread, fry.
