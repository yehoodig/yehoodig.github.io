Garden Vegetable Soup

Notes:

A Weight Watcher's Recipe. 1 cup = 0 points.

You will need:

  • 2/3 Sliced Carrots
  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 Cups fat free broth (Beef, chicken or vegetable)
  • 1 1/2 Cups diced Green cabbage
  • 1/2 green Beans
  • 1 Tomato paste
  • 1/2 dried basil
  • 1/4 oregano
  • 1/4 salt
  • 1/2 zucchini

Procedure:

In large saucepan, sprayed w/non-stick cooking spray, saute carrots, onions and garlic over low heat until softened, about 5 min.


Add broth, cabbage, beans, tomato paste, basil, oregano, and salt; bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer covered, about 15 min.


Stir in zucchini and heat 3-4 min. Serve hot.

Makes 4 1-cup servings.
