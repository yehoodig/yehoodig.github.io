Oatmeal

Notes:

Oatmeal even the kids will eat.

You will need:

  • 1/2 Cup Oatmeal per person
  • 1/8 tsp Cinnamon per person
  • 1 Tbsp Butter per person
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar per person
  • 1/8 Cup Raisins per person
  • 1 Cup Water per person

Procedure:

Dump everything in a pot of appropriate size, heat over medium to medium high heat.
Stir and serve.
