Old Fashioned Macaroni and Cheese

Notes:

Old old old betty crocker cook book from Nancy

You will need:

  • 6-7 ouncesmacaroni 2cps
  • 2 Tablespoonsgrated onion
  • 1 teaspoonsalt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3 Cups shredded american cheese 3/4lb
  • 2 Cups thin white sauce
  • 1 Tablespoonbutter

Procedure:

heat oven to 375°F.
cook macaroni put half in casarole dish. Sprinkle with half the onion
salt pepper and cheese. repeat. Pour white sauce over top. Dot with butter.
Cover and bake 30min uncover and bake 15min.
