Peanut Curry Pilaf

Notes:

I had some peanuts lying around

You will need:

  • 1/2 Cup Peanuts
  • 1 Scoop Rice
  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 3 Tbsps Peanut oil

Procedure:

Sautee peanuts and spices in peanut oil. Add rice and continue sauteing
until rice turns kind of whitish, and is well coated with oil. Add chicken broth.
Cover and simmer until done, about 20 min.
