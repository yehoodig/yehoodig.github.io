Pork Slices with Mandarin Chinese Vegetables

Notes:

Off of the Vip stir-fry vegetables package.

You will need:

  • 1 14oz package stir-fry vegetables
  • 8 oz lean pork, thin sliced
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce

Procedure:

Combine pork and soy sauce in medium bowl. Heat oil in wok or skillet over medium high heat. Add pork; stir-fry 1 min. Add vegetables; stir-fry 3-4 minutes. Serve hot.

Makes 4 servings.
