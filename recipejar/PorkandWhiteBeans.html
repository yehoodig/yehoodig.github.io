Pork and White Beans

Notes:

McConnel Tradition

You will need:

  • 1 piece Pork (a roast, or something with a bone, anything that has plenty of fat, and flavor)
  • 1 pkg Navy Beans
  • 1 small onion minced
  • Coriander
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Maybe some garlic

Procedure:

Put everything into the crock pot.
