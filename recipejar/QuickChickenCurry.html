Quick Chicken Curry

Notes:

Just got lucky

You will need:

  • 1 can Cream of Chicken soup
  • 1-2 tsps Curry powder
  • 1 lb Cubed chicken
  • 1 diced potato
  • 1 diced onion

Procedure:

Mix the soup and curry powder in a large deep pan.
Add vegetables and chicken simmer over medium low
until chicken is cooked through.

Serve over rice.
