Rhubarb Cream Delight Dessert

Notes:

You will need:

  • 1 Cup flour
  • 1/2 Cup butter
  • 1 1/4 Cups sugar
  • 3 Cups rhubarb cut to 1/2in
  • 1 Tablespoon flour
  • 12 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Procedure:

Crust: mix 1 cup flour 1/4 cup sugar, and 1/2 cup butter pat into 10in pie plate, set aside.
Rhubarb layer: combine rhubarb, 1/2 cup sugar and 1 Tbsp flour, toss put in crust.
bake at 375°Ffor about15min.
Cream Layer: Beat together cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar until fluffy. beat in eggs one at a time then pour over hot rhubarb.
Bake at 375°Ffor about 30min.

Topping: combine 8 oz sour cream, 2 Tbsp sugar and 1 tsp vanilla, spread over hot pie

chill
