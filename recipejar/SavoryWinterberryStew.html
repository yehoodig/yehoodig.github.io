Savory Winterberry Stew

Notes:

Something I tried. Erin thinks it's inspiring; not fabulous, but second helpings were
had by all and a discussion of revisions ensued.

You will need:

  • 1 lb Ground Turkey
  • 3/4 Cup Barley
  • 2-3 Tbsps Olive oil
  • 1/2 Cup White wine
  • 2 minced Onions
  • Salt
  • white pepper
  • cinnamon
  • ground cloves
  • crushed bay leaves
  • 1 Cup Chopped Cranberries
  • 1 Potato

Procedure:

Roast the barley in a 300° oven for about 15min, or until a nice color.
Add roasted barley to a large pot. Add Olive oil, onions, cranberries, spices and white wine.
Cook over medium-high heat about 15min or until onions are well done.
Add ground turkey. Cook until browned. Add potatoes, and 2 cups water.
Cook over medium heat until potatoes are done. Add another cup water, heat as desired.

Serve
