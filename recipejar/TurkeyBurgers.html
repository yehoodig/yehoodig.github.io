Turkey Burgers

Notes:

An Erin original

You will need:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 Cup oatmeal
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Worcestershire sauce

Procedure:

Mix turkey, egg and oatmeal.
Add seasonings to taste.
Pan fry or grill as desired.
