Wheat Berry and Shrimp Salad with Thai Vinaigrette

Notes:

Weight Watchers. 4 Servings. 3 pts per 1 1/4 cup serving.

You will need:

  • 3/4 Cup Wheat berries
  • 6 oz Shrimp
  • 3 Tbsps lime juice
  • 1 1/2 Tbsps fish sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 med seedless cuke peeled, halved and chopped
  • 6 radishes, small
  • 1/4 Cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 Tbsps mint leaves, chopped

Procedure:


Place wheat berries in a med pot cover with 3 inches of water and simmer over med-high heat, stirring once or twice. Cover, reduce heat to med-low and simmer until tender (50 min). Drain and let cool.

Rinse pot, and fill halfway with water. Bring to a boil and add shrimp. Reduce heat and simmer until shrimp turn pink. (2 min) Drain, rinse and chop.

mix lime juice, fish sauce, and sugar.

Toss it all together.
